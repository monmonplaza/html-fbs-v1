Recognizing the Achievements of Our On-the-Job and Continuing Studies Trainees in Full-Stack Development

  • Events
  • August 4, 2025

We proudly recognize and celebrate the successful completion of the On-the-Job Training (OJT) by the students from Bicol University – Polangui Campus, as well as the Continuing Studies Training completed by a student from Cavite State University.

Despite the limited training period of 240 hours, these interns made significant strides in becoming proficient full-stack developers—thanks to their commitment and the dedicated mentorship of our expert developers and trainers.

Our trainees applied their classroom knowledge to real-life development projects, beginning with the fundamentals of frontend design and moving toward backend development and system integration. Their learning outcomes included:

  • Converting design mockups into actual web pages using HTML and CSS
  • Applying dynamic behaviors using JavaScript
  • Building responsive layouts efficiently using Tailwind CSS
  • Developing user interfaces and frontend applications using ReactJS
  • Managing application data with Props, State, and Store Context in ReactJS
  • Performing backend operations using MySQL, including CRUD
  • Handling CORS configuration and integrating RESTful APIs
  • Participating in real-world ReactJS project development

We are incredibly proud to be part of the growth and development of these aspiring professionals. With hands-on experience, industry-level tools, and collaborative projects, our interns developed not only technical expertise but also essential soft skills such as communication, critical thinking, and adaptability.

To our On-the-Job and Continuing Studies Trainees—congratulations on your achievements! Your commitment and determination have laid the groundwork for a promising future in the tech industry. Thank you for choosing Frontline Business Solutions, Inc. as your training partner.
